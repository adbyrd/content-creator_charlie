# Rent-A-Chicken Co.

## Company Details

**Rent-A-Chicken Co.**
Seasonal backyard chicken rentals that provide families with egg-laying hens, a coop, and feed for several months.

**Target Audience**
Suburban families interested in farm-to-table food and sustainable living but not ready to permanently own chickens.

**Marketing Objective**
Promote spring and summer chicken rental packages and increase seasonal bookings.
